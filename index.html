  • MUNICIPALITY OF LI-LOAN

    • LILO-AN, officially the Municipality of Liloan, is a 1st class municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 153,197 people. Liloan lies within Metro Cebu.

  • PAROLA SA LILO-AN

    • The beautiful lighthouse that is the icon of the municipality of Liloan was built more than 100 years ago in 1904 to give light to the sea and shore for fishers. Parola is the Bisaya word for lighthouse. It was built during the American occupation under Governor-General William Howard Taft. The Parola sa Liloan is also known as Bagacay Point Lighthouse.

  • ROSQUILLOS FESTIVAL

    • The Rosquillos Festival in named after the Liloan's famous delicacy, Rosquillos, produced by Titay's Liloan Rosquillos and Delicacies, Inc. The festival in honor of Liloan's patron saint, St. Ferdinand El Rey and his fiesta is celebrated every May 29-30. in Liloan, Philippines. ( near Liloan, Philippines)
